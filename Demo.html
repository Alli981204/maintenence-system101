    Electricity Generation and Distribution Department

    What we do?

    We distribute electricity to residential, commercial and industrial customers in Cape Town and provide the link between Eskom and electricity consumers.

    We also construct and maintain the equipment that transforms the power supply for the consumers’ needs and supply public lighting.

    Our department also provides support services, including vending and billing systems, as well as the operation of call centres and load control systems.
